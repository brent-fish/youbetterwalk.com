What We Stand For

Three things we will not compromise on.

01
Enrichment

A walk is more than a bathroom break. It's a chance to sniff, stretch, and engage. Every outing is done with intention because even a walk around the block could be the best part of their day.

02
Affirmation

Our pets are present and accepting of our humanity, and we follow their example. Anyone with empathy and an open heart is a welcome and cherished client, and every pet deserves an opportunity to be served.

03
Affection

As pet owners and caretakers, our job is ensuring pets know they are loved. Your pet is family and a fully-fledged individual with their own personality, and we'll treat them that way every single visit.

Our Walking Policy

One dog. One walk. Every time.

Every walk we provide is a private, dedicated session for your dog. No distractions or divided attention. In multi-dog households, each dog walks individually. This is not a limitation but by design. Your dog gets the undivided focus, safety, and care they deserve. Every single time. If you prefer back-to-back walks in a multiple-dog house, we are happy to offer a discount.

The Team

Meet Brent

Brent

Owner

Upper Arlington parent. Published Writer.
Professional nice person.

Cat-Raised, Dog-Owned • Over 25 years of experience with both.

Hello there! I've been a resident of Columbus, Ohio for over 20 years. Originally from NW Ohio, I have experience being owned and loved by cats and dogs for a majority of my life. After walking and caring for dogs of all ages for more than a decade, it became clear that people are still looking for someone reliable and not part of an app or a chain to fill in the gaps of care for their pets. It felt like a natural fit to fill it.
