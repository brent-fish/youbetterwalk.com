Policies & Terms.

Please read each section carefully. By signing below, you confirm that you have read and understood each item.

One Dog Per Walk. All dog walking services are conducted as private, one-on-one sessions. In multi-dog households, each dog walks individually. This is a standing policy and is not subject to exception.

Cancellation. We ask for at least 48 hours' notice for any cancellation or schedule change. Cancellations with less than 48 hours' notice may be charged 50% of the scheduled visit. Cancellations with less than 24 hours' notice, same-day cancellations, and no-shows are charged 100% of the scheduled visit. Holiday visit cancellations are charged at 100% regardless of notice given.

Pet Health Disclosure. You represent that you have disclosed all known health conditions, behavioral history, medications, allergies, and any history of aggression or unpredictable behavior. You Better Walk LLC is not liable for incidents arising from undisclosed health or behavioral conditions.

Emergency Veterinary Authorization. You authorize You Better Walk LLC to seek emergency veterinary care for your pet if you and your emergency contact cannot be reached and we believe immediate care is necessary. You agree to reimburse all reasonable veterinary expenses incurred on your behalf. We will contact your designated emergency veterinarian unless instructed otherwise.

Liability. You are fully responsible for the behavior of your pet. If your pet bites, injures, or causes damage to a person or property during a service visit, you agree to be solely responsible for all resulting costs and claims. You Better Walk LLC carries general liability insurance and animal bailee coverage appropriate for these services. Our liability for any claim shall not exceed the amount paid for the specific service visit in question.

Home Access & Keys. Any keys, codes, or access credentials provided are stored securely and used solely for service delivery. They are never shared with unauthorized parties. Upon termination of services, you may request the return or destruction of any physical keys within 7 days.

Termination. Either party may terminate this agreement at any time. You Better Walk LLC reserves the right to immediately discontinue services if false information has been provided, a pet presents an undisclosed danger, or a client engages in abusive or harassing conduct.

Holiday & Peak Season Rates. Services provided during the full week surrounding major holidays (New Year's, Easter, Memorial Day, July 4th, Labor Day, Thanksgiving, Christmas) are subject to a holiday surcharge communicated in advance. Peak season rates (Memorial Day through Labor Day) may apply to new service arrangements. You will always be notified before any rate adjustment takes effect.
