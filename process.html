Our Process

How this works.

From the first message to an ongoing care relationship, here is what you should expect: a clear path to someone your pet will look forward to seeing.

01

Getting Started

Reach out.

Use the contact form on the site or send a direct email. Tell us a little about your pet - their name, their species, any quirks worth knowing about. There are no wrong answers and nothing too small to mention. The more you share upfront, the better the first conversation goes.

Response time: Within 24 hours, usually sooner.
02

First Connection

We connect.

We'll follow up to learn more and answer any questions you have. This is also when we share a short pet profile form - basic health history, emergency contacts, vet information, routine, and behavioral notes. It takes about ten minutes and helps us arrive at the meet and greet already knowing your pet on paper.

Note: We may ask follow-up questions about specific needs before scheduling a meet and greet. This is normal - we want to make sure we're the right fit before either of us commits to anything.
03

The Meeting

Meet and greet.

We come to you at home, with your pet present. We'll talk about routines, your pet's personality, and what good care looks like for your needs. There is no pitch and no pressure. Just like a visit from a neighbor. If it feels right for both of us, we move forward. If either side isn't feeling it, we part ways with no hard feelings.

Always free. Always at your home. Never rushed. This meeting is as much for your pet to meet us as it is for us to meet them.
04

Making It Official

Paperwork and access.

Once we've agreed we're a good match, we finalize the pet profile, sign the service agreement, and handle key or access code exchange. We keep access information in secure storage and it never leaves the service relationship.

Fully insured. You Better Walk carries liability insurance through Pet Care Insurance, covering general liability, professional liability, and care, custody, and control of animals in our care. Proof of coverage available on request.
05

Day One

The first visit.

The first visit is always done with a little extra attention and patience. New places, new routines, new person - pets notice all of it. We take it at their pace. After the visit, you get a photo report card on how it went: what they did, how they felt, anything worth knowing.

Every visit includes: a photo report card sent directly to you when we're done.
06

The Long Game

Ongoing care.

Over time we learn your pet's moods, their favorite routes, the signs that something is off. Scheduling, adjustments, and questions go straight to us, not an app.

Direct line, always. You will always be communicating with the person who cared for your pet.

Ready When You Are

Let's talk about
your pet.

The first step is just a message. Tell us about your animal, your schedule, your situation. We’ll take it from there.
